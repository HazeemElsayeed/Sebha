سبحة

اللهمَّ إنك عفوٌّ تُحبُّ العفوَ فاعفُ عنِّي

Allahumma innaka 'afuwwun tuhibbul-'afwa, fa'fu 'anni

O Allah, You are Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me

لا إلهَ إلاّ اللّهُ وحْـدَهُ لا شَـريكَ لهُ، لهُ المُـلْكُ ولهُ الحَمْـد، وهُوَ على كُلّ شَيءٍ قَدير

Laa ilaha ‘illa Allaahu wahdahu la shareeka lahu, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu wa huwa ala kulli shayin qadeer

There is none worthy of worship in truth except Allaah Alone, He has no partners, to Him belongs the Dominion and for Him is all praise, and He is capable of all things

سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ عَدَدَ خَلْقِهِ، وَرِضَا نَفْسِهِ، وَزِنَةَ عَرْشِهِ وَمِدَادَ كَلِمَاتِهِ

subhan allh wabihamdih eadad khalqihi, warida nafsihi, wazinat earshih wamidad kalimatih

Glory is to Allah and praise is to Him, by the quantity of His creation, by His Pleasure, by the weight of His Throne, and by the extent of His Words

اللهم صّلِ وسَلّمْ عَلۓِ نَبِيْنَامُحَمد

allahuma sali wasalim ealaa nabiina muhamad

God blessings and peace upon our Prophet Muhammad

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ الْعَظيمَ الَّذِي لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ هُوَ الْحَيُّ القَيّوُمُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيهِ

Astaghfirullah al-'Adheem al-ladhi la ilaha illa Huwal-Hayyul-Qayyum wa atubu ilaih.

I seek the forgiveness of Allah the Mighty, Whom there is none worthy except Him, the Living, The Eternal, and I repent to Him

اللَّهُمَّ إنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي ظُلْمًا كَثِيرًا، ولَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إلَّا أنْتَ، فَاغْفِرْ لي مَغْفِرَةً مِن عِندِكَ، وارْحَمْنِي، إنَّكَ أنْتَ الغَفُورُ الرَّحِيمُ

alllahumm 'inni zalamt nafsi zulman kathiran, wla yaghfir aldhdhunub 'illa anta, faghfir li maghfiratan min eindika, warhamni, 'innak ant alghafur alrrahim

O Allah, I have greatly wronged myself, and no one forgives sins except You, so grant me forgiveness from You and have mercy on me, You are the Forgiving and the Merciful One

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي

rabi aghfir li

Lord forgive me

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ

'astaghfir allah

I ask God for forgiveness

اللَّهُمَّ أنْتَ رَبِّي لا إلَهَ إلَّا أنْتَ، خَلَقْتَنِي وأنا عَبْدُكَ، وأنا علَى عَهْدِكَ ووَعْدِكَ ما اسْتَطَعْتُ، أعُوذُ بكَ مِن شَرِّ ما صَنَعْتُ، أبُوءُ لكَ بنِعْمَتِكَ عَلَيَّ، وأَبُوءُ لكَ بذَنْبِي فاغْفِرْ لِي؛ فإنَّه لا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إلَّا أنْتَ

alllahumm ant rabbi la 'ilah 'illa anta, khalaqtani wa'ana eabduka, wa'ana elaa eahdik wwaedik ma astataetu, aeudh bk min sharr ma sanaetu, abu' lk bniematik ealaya, w'abu' lk bdhanbi faghfir li; f'innah la yaghfir aldhdhunub 'illa ant

O Allah! You are my Rabb. There is no true god except You. You have created me, and I am Your slave, and I hold to Your Covenant as far as I can. I seek refuge in You from the evil of what I have done. I acknowledge the favors that You have bestowed upon me, and I confess my sins. Pardon me, for none but You has the power to pardon

سُبْحانَ اللهِ، والْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، ولا إلَهَ إلَّا اللَّهُ، واللَّهُ أكْبَرُ

subhan allah w alhamd allah w la 'iilah 'iilaa allah w allah 'akbar

Allah is free from imperfection ,all praise is due to Allah ,there is no true god except Allah and Allah is the Greatest
